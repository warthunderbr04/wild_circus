"Wild Circus"

The Diorio's "Bikers of the extreme"

Brazilian performer Daniel Chrystian Diorio is a daredevil. His unique Globe of Death Act defies all odds, combining precision riding and death defying stunts to present one of the worlds most thrilling Speciality Acts of performing arts. Hear the roar of stunt motorbikes as four riders perform various manoeuvres inside a specially designed globe.
